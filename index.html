Aren's Photo Website

Hello, I am Aren Francis, this is a webpage that I made using just HTML and no CSS. This webpage contains all the stuff we learned from Nicole Alexander in the ITEC 240 class so far.
I'm going to use this website to showcase some photos that I took over the years as a hobby photographer as well as talk a little bit about the hobby. I hope you all enjoy your stay!


Why Nature photography?

I've always liked photography growing up and nature has a number of positive effects on me:

  • Spending time in nature reduces my anxiety.
  • The air is cleaner and I feel more revitalised.
  • Walking in nature get's the blood flowing and is good for the heart.
  • Seeing rare wildlife can instantly lift my mood.
